Team Profile

Denise Freiburger:

MBTI personality type: INTJ

Strengths:

• You have good interpersonal skills

• You are able to work with your team to resolve problems

• You are able to communicate effectively during teamwork

• You are able to manage the different tasks, goals and other aspects of teamwork

• You are good at setting team goals

• You are skilled at planning and coordinating with teammates

Potential Strengths:

• You are sometimes able to resolve conflict with teammates

Limitations:

• You seem to prefer individual work over teamwork

Learning style:

Solitary

The results show I am strong in completing solitary tasks that challenge me, but weak when completing repetitive or ‘boring’ tasks. I can lose focus quickly if I feel a task isn’t ‘useful’ or ‘signifcant’.

I need to be aware of a tendancy to want to ‘do it myself’ instead of seeking assistance from team members. I need to remind myself that others have different ways of operating and that doesn’t always mean they’re less effective.

A team should (where possible) be a blend of different personality types. Difference can be useful as long as the team as a whole is open to listening to the ideas and thoughts of the other members.

Zhaohe Lin:

MBTI personality type: ISTJ

Strengths:

• Honest and direct

• Strong willed and dutiful

• Very responsible

• Calm and practical

• Create and enforce order

Potential Strengths:

• Able to apply myself to a variety of situations, picking up and applying new data and grasping the details of challenging situations as a matter of course.

Limitations:

• I’m a bit insensitive and might hurt sensitive type feelings unintentionally.

Learning style: Solitary

Test results show that I’m a more introverted individual that prefers solitary activities and gets exhausted by social interaction. I’m also highly practical, pragmatic and down-to-earth. I have a strong ability to focus on what is happening or has already happened. I focus on objectivity and rationality, prioritising logic over emotions.

I am highly self-disciplined and take responsibility for the tasks I perform. I work hard and stay focused on my goal which will prove me to be a valuable member of the team. My weakness is my introverted personality so that I will need to manage my interpersonal relations by speaking up at times when necessary and participating in group discussions. I need to ask questions when I’m in doubt and lend a helping hand to team members that struggle.

Jennafer Allen:

MBTI personality type: ISTJ-T

Strengths:

• Honest and direct: manage situations with honesty and as simply as possible.

• Strong willed and dutiful: reliable and focused on seeing results.

• Very responsible: loyal to my word and any obligation I am required to meet.

• Calm and practical: works in a logical manner.

• Create and enforce order: creates structure in chaos.

• Jack-of-all-trades: easy to learn new information and then apply it in certain situations.

Potential Strengths:

• I am a goal orientated person that can be relied upon to achieve tasks and help to focus the team’s outcomes.

Limitations:

• I am a factual decision maker that is resistant to others’ ideas, even if my own is wrong.

Learning style:

Independent

My test results have indicated that I favor practical logic in my decision making. I have a strong belief in rules and order and the integrity of always following them. I am goals orientated and will give everything to a task that I am given until it is complete. I will aid this team by being a dedicated member who will strive for excellence and the completion of our goals.

My weaknesses include the inability to manage indecisiveness or decision based on feelings not fact. As such, when working with Alpha One I should remain cognizant of this when working as a team. I can do this by enabling a positive environment where everyone’s beliefs are equal to my own and remain calm during the decision-making process.
